# Deep Research Skill

I am a specialized skill for performing structured, multi-phase research. I help you avoid "surface-level" AI answers by forcing a two-stage process: outline creation and deep per-item investigation.

## What I do
1. **Outline Generation**: Create a research plan with specific items (e.g., companies, technologies) and fields (e.g., pricing, tech stack).
2. **Deep Investigation**: Use parallel web search agents to gather evidence for every cell in the research matrix.
3. **Report Synthesis**: Compile raw data into a structured Markdown report with citations.

## When to use me
- When you need to compare multiple technologies, frameworks, or competitors.
- For academic literature reviews or market trend analysis.
- When the task requires "finding everything" rather than just a quick summary.

## Rules & Constraints
- Always confirm the research outline with the user before starting the `/deep-research` phase.
- Use the `websearch` tool for information gathering.
- Ensure all findings include source URLs for verification.
